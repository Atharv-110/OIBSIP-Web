Biography

Elon Musk is the charismatic co-founder and CEO of electric car maker Tesla and rocket manufacturer SpaceX. Born and raised in South Africa, Musk spent time in Canada before finally moving to the U.S. Educated at the University of Pennsylvania in physics, Musk started getting his feet wet as a serial tech entrepreneur with early successes like Zip2 and X.com. He was instrumental in creating the company that became PayPal. Musk has behaved in eccentric ways from time to time and has identified himself as having Asperger's syndrome.
